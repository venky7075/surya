Mental Health Awareness

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and behave in daily life. Good mental health helps people cope with stress, relate to others, and make decisions.

It is important to take care of mental health just like physical health. Regular self-care, seeking support, and maintaining a balanced lifestyle contribute to overall well-being.

Tips for Good Mental Health

  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Engage in physical activities
  • Get enough sleep
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation
  • Seek professional help when needed
